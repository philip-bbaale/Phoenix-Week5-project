Pregnancy.

Welcome to your beautiful journey

Pregnancy is an amazing experience. You get to experience changes in your body. They could be uncomfortable, but that is normal since there is two of you now in one body. As your baby grows by the day, it is important that you have a balanced diet that will help you and your baby stay healthy before it is born.

The begining

The first 3 months of your journey are here!! Your body is producing new sets of hormones that ensure the survival of your baby. The hormones can cause cravings, mood swings, and change in body size. Do not worry, this is normal. In this phase, there are a few dietary prescriptions that will make this initial stages a lot easier.

The Baby bump!!

Your baby is starting to show. Isn't this exciting. in this stage, you get to see the progress of your pregnacy. At this stage, the baby is rapidly growing. You probably feel hungrier and more fatigued. The constant need to eat and sleep could be unhealthy for you and your baby. It is important that the diet for which you settle is healthy for both of you

It is almost time!!

The last three months are probably the most hectic. Not only are you fatigued, but your baby is at its highest growth and development rate. Within this phase, the baby's organs are finally fully coming together to form a healthy human being. Even with the anxiety around birth, it is important that you maintain a healthy diet.
