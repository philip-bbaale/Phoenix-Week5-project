Diabetes is a medical condition where by the blood sugar , also known as blood glucose, is too high.

There are different kinds of diabetes i.e;

  • Diabetes type 1
  • Diabetes type 2
  • Prediabetes

Diabetes type 1

Type 1 diabetes is an autoimmune disease where by the immune system attacks and destroys cells in the pancreas, where insulin is made.

This later results in a persons blood sugar hitting the roof.

To avoid high blood sugar one should avoid the following;

Processed sugar
Energy drinks
Unhealthy breakfast foods

For a healthy and ntutritious diet, you should consider having the following included in each meal;

Dark green leafy veggies
Fruits wih high fibre
Healthy oil foods
Fat free yogurt

Diabetes type 2

Type 2 diabetes is a lifelong disease that keeps your body from using insulin the way it should.

People with type 2 diabetes are said to have insulin resistance.

The following are the top 3 foods which should be avoided by people with this condition;

High cholestrol foods
High transfat foods
Foods with added sugar
