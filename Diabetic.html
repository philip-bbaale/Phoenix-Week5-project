Diet tips

  • Keep track of the amount of carbs you eat and drink in a day as the can affect your blood sugar level.
  • Get involved in doing physical activities to improve blood sugar levels and lower blood pressure.
  • Drink water before, after and during exercises to stay well hydrated.
  • Eat regular meals -about 6 per day.
